UX Writing Samples

Selected examples of reframing technical logic into action-oriented UI copy.

Focus: Transitioning from formal "Big Bank" language to accessible business terms.

ContextOriginal / Non-InclusiveReframed (Inclusive)
Business Setup"Are you a Sole Proprietor or an Entity?""Are you working on your own or as a registered company?"
Credit Access"Provide 3 years of audited P&L statements.""Provide your business income records from the last 3 years."
Invoice Status"Invoice #102 is currently delinquent.""Payment for Invoice #102 is past its due date."
Loan Eligibility"You lack sufficient collateral for this product.""To qualify for this loan, you'll need to provide an asset as security."
Tax Filing"Execute your VAT reconciliation.""Check your sales tax totals for the month."

Focus: Removing technical hierarchy and physical/spatial metaphors.

ContextOriginal / Non-InclusiveReframed (Inclusive)
User Roles"Grant Master Access to this user.""Give Full Permissions to this user."
System Rules"Apply a hard-stop to these parameters.""Set a strict limit for these settings."
Default Settings"Revert to factory-fresh settings.""Restore original settings."
Data Migration"Kill the old process before starting.""Stop the previous task before starting the new one."
Display"Enable Dark Mode for better vision.""Switch to Dark Mode for high-contrast viewing."

Focus: Neutralizing corporate jargon for diverse stakeholders.

ContextComplex / Non-InclusiveReframed (Inclusive)
Compliance"Is your firm KYC compliant?""Has your business verified its identity and documents?"
Audit Logs"View the trail of breadcrumbs for this edit.""View the history of changes for this record."
Risk Thresholds"Set your appetite for market volatility.""Choose how much market change your business can handle."
Whitelabeling"Whitelabel this dashboard for your clients.""Add your own branding to this dashboard."
Internal Controls"Establish a Four-Eyes Principle for approvals.""Require two different people to approve this action."

Focus: Moving from aggressive command language to collaborative status language.

ContextOriginal / Non-InclusiveReframed (Inclusive)
Task Assignment"You have been assigned as the owner.""You are now responsible for this task."
Process Errors"The system suffered a fatal crash.""The system stopped unexpectedly."
Resource Planning"Analyze your human capital requirements.""Review your team's staffing needs."
Inventory"Purge all dead-stock from the warehouse.""Remove unsold items from your inventory records."
Onboarding"Please complete the vetting process.""Please complete the information review."

Focus: Replacing "Master/Slave" or "Parent/Child" technical structures.

ContextComplex / Non-InclusiveReframed (Inclusive)
Account Structure"Manage Parent/Child accounts.""Manage Main and Sub-accounts."
Data Inheritance"Child objects inherit traits from the Parent.""New entries follow the rules set by the main category."
Access Tiers"Only Super-Admins can see this.""Access is restricted to authorized account owners."
Shared Folders"This folder is orphaned.""This folder is unlinked from a main category."
Drafting"Discard the dummy content.""Remove the placeholder text."

Case Study: Compliance Redesign

Overview: A major MSME platform experienced 42% drop-off during compliance steps. Analysis showed functionality wasn't the issue: the barrier was system-centric terminology and non-literal metaphors.

Strategy Applied:

  • Neuro-Accessible Writing (Direct and literal)
  • Operational Clarity (Functional descriptions)
  • Green UX (Reduced text weight)
82% Completion Rate (Up from 58%)
4% Jargon Support Tickets (Down from 22%)
WCAG AAA Accessibility Rating
